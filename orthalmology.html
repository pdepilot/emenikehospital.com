Advanced Vision Correction

Our state-of-the-art vision correction procedures can help you achieve clear, natural vision and reduce or eliminate your dependence on glasses or contact lenses.

LASIK Surgery

Laser-assisted procedure that reshapes the cornea to correct nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism.

PRK (Photorefractive Keratectomy)

Surface laser treatment ideal for patients with thin corneas or certain occupational requirements.

ICL (Implantable Collamer Lens)

Implantable contact lenses for patients who are not candidates for laser vision correction.

Presbyopia Correction

Advanced solutions for age-related near vision loss, including multifocal lenses and corneal inlays.

Advanced Retinal Treatments

Our retinal specialists provide comprehensive care for complex retinal conditions using the latest diagnostic and treatment technologies.

Intravitreal Injections

Medication injections to treat macular degeneration, diabetic retinopathy, and retinal vein occlusions.

Laser Photocoagulation

Precise laser treatment for diabetic retinopathy, retinal tears, and other retinal conditions.

Vitrectomy Surgery

Microsurgical procedure to remove vitreous gel and treat retinal detachments, macular holes, and diabetic vitreous hemorrhage.

Retinal Imaging

Advanced OCT, fluorescein angiography, and ultra-widefield imaging for precise diagnosis and monitoring.
