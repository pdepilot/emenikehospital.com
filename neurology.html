About Our Neurology Department

At Emenike Hospital, our Neurology Department is dedicated to providing comprehensive care for patients with disorders of the nervous system. Our team of board-certified neurologists and neurosurgeons utilize the latest diagnostic tools and treatment approaches to deliver exceptional care.

We specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, nerves, and muscles. Our multidisciplinary approach ensures that each patient receives personalized care tailored to their specific needs.

Expert Diagnosis

Advanced diagnostic capabilities including EEG, EMG, and neuroimaging.

Comprehensive Treatment

From medication management to advanced surgical interventions.

Specialized Care

Subspecialty expertise in stroke, epilepsy, movement disorders, and more.

Advanced Technology & Diagnostics

We utilize state-of-the-art technology for accurate diagnosis and effective treatment of neurological conditions.

3T MRI & fMRI

High-resolution magnetic resonance imaging for detailed visualization of brain structure and function.

Digital EEG

Advanced electroencephalography for monitoring brain activity and diagnosing seizure disorders.

CT Angiography

Computed tomography angiography for detailed imaging of blood vessels in the brain and neck.
